Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shipping policy?

We offer worldwide shipping with standard delivery times ranging from 5-10 business days.

Can I return a book?

Yes, returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase, as long as the book is in its original condition.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use this to check your shipment status.
